T1: RECURRENT TRAINING MODULE



Lesson Overview


During this lesson, we have one part with a short LOFT Training and another part with different Non-Normal Procedures.


Exercices


  • ATA 34: NAV
  • ATA 70: ENG
  • TCAS
  • WINDSHEAR
  • Eng. Failure during climb / Cruise
  • Eng. Failure at Takeoff
  • OEI App, go around and Landing


    • Training objective :


  • Good ECAM/Procedures handling & Failure Management.
  • Good CRM, task sharing and crew coordination during all phases of flight.
  • Strict compliance with stabilization criteria in approach
